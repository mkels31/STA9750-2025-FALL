Success in India

Netflix is continuing to expand in India, the world’s most populated country. With 691 Hindu-language films, India has become a major hub for original content. Among them, “The Railway Men - The Untold Story Of Bhopal 1984” stands out as the most popular, remaining in India’s Top 10 list for 30 weeks. Over the past three years, viewership has shown srtong growth, with 2.4 billion hours viewed in 2023, 4.6 billion in 2024, and 4.2 billion hours already logged in 2025 with more time still to go.
